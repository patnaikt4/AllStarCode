# CV Models Research

## Overview

Research into computer vision (CV) and video-understanding models that could later provide visual context (movement, facial cues, tonality) for the LLM feedback pipeline (MVP2).

## How It Works

- **Goal:** Compare options (e.g. action/gesture recognition, face/expression detection, video-language models) on ease of use, cost, and relevance to instructor teaching (talking head, screen share, gestures).
- **Deliverable:** A short report with a comparison table and 1–2 recommended options for a resource-constrained nonprofit context.
- **Location:** Findings and recommendations to be written in this doc (or linked) by the team.

## Future Integration

- The chosen model(s) will feed visual signals into the feedback pipeline so the LLM can offer feedback on presence, engagement, and delivery (MVP2).
